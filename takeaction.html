Interested in joining the movement towards ending gun violence?

Make your voice heard and be the catalyst for significant change in your community and America as a whole.


Learn more about the reputable anti-gun organizations below to find more ways to get directly involved in the movement. Below are resources that share the same goal as Bravery, Not Bullets.

March For Our Lives

March For Our Lives is a student-led movement and organization with the primary goal of ending gun violence in America through protests and campaigns to ensure the safety of students and communities.

Everytown for Gun Safety

Everytown for Gun Safety is a nonprofit organization dedicated to advocating for common-sense gun safety measures and gun control policies in the United States.

Guns Down America

Guns Down America is a nonprofit organization that aims to create a safer environment by addressing issues related to gun control and advocating for gun safety measures to reduce gun violence in America

Sandy Hook Promise

Sandy Hook Promise is a nonprofit organization founded by family members who lost loved ones in the Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting in Newtown, Connecticut, in 2012. Their main goal is to prevent gun violence and protect families from experiencing similar tragedies.
