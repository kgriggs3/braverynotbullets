Bravery, Not Bullets believes that everyone in America should lead a safe life without worrying about becoming a victim of gun violence.

Explore our website to discover more about our organization, Bravery, Not Bullets, resources, insights, and statistics to learn more about the matter of gun violence, and how you can join us in starting the important conversation of gun violence in America.


The Challenge

Gun violence is a multifaceted problem that touches the lives of countless individuals and communities across the country. While there are many perspectives on this issue and fierce debates on how it should be solved,it is undeniable that it has far-reaching and devastating consequences for families, schools, workplaces, and society at large.


Our Approach

We are committed to providing you with accurate information, helpful resources, and various viewpoints to spread awareness andcombat the growing issue of gun violence. Together, by informing ourselves, we can all promote awareness and drive change to find a solution to this complex issue.
