35,000+ people have died in America in 2023 so far due to gun violence.

Of those who passed...

  • 246 were children

  • 1,574 were teens

  • 597 were victims in mass shootings

  • 960 were unintentional

Bravery, Not Bullets believes that everyone in America should lead a safe life without worrying about becoming a victim of gun violence.

Explore our website to discover more about our organization, Bravery, Not Bullets, resources, insights, and statistics to learn more about the matter of gun violence, and how you can join us in starting the important conversation of gun violence in America.

Our website is meant to spread awareness to Americans about the growing issue of gun violence in America, but also inform non-Americans on gun violence in America to prevent misinformation from being spread on the issue.

Together, by informing ourselves, we can all make a difference and spark change.
